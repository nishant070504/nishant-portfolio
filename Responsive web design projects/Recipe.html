Bread and Omelette

This dish is a perfect breakfast option to start your day.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp oil
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add salt and pepper, and whisk well.
  2. Heat a frying pan over medium heat and add a little oil.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and cook until set.
  4. Toast the bread slices with butter in the same pan until golden brown.
  5. Place the cooked omelette between two slices of bread and spread mayonnaise on top.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy!
